Steak Frites

A classic French staple that has made it's way into the home kithcen. Steak Frites is easy as it is delicous. Juicy steak and savory fries can't be beat.

Ingrediants

  • Your choice of cut of steak. (I choose flank)
  • Russet potatos, about 1 per person
Lemon Thyme Aioli
  • 1/4 c of Mayo
  • 1 tbsp of Dijon mustard
  • 2 garlic cloves (grated or minced to a paste)
  • Zest of 1 lemon, 1-2tsp of lemon juice
  • 1-2 springs of fresh thyme
  • Salt and Pepper to taste

Steps

  1. First take out the steak and let it get to room temp, about 30 minutes. also cut the potatoes into preferred fry size and let soak.
  2. While waiting make the ailoi. Combine mayo, dijon mustard, garlic, zest, lemon juice, and thyme in a bowl. Salt and pepper to taste. Put in the fridge to chill.
  3. In a hot pan sear the steak on both sids, flipping untill steak ahs reached desired doness. Let rest while you finish the frites.
  4. Take the fries out of the water and pat them dry. in a heavy duty saucepan or dutch oven heat canola oil (or any frying oil) upto 350f. Put the fries in the oil for about 3-5 minutes.
  5. When done put fries in a bowl and add seaoning while still hot.
  6. Plate and enjoy!
