Shrimp and Lobster Sauce

Description

Shrimp and lobster sauce is a warm and savory cantonese classic. Enjoyd over rice, or with well.... lobster. Despite the name it does not contain any lobster, however pairs well with it. Made up of simple yet delicous ingrediants, it's an easy way to mix up dinner at home.

Ingrediants

  • 1lb of shrimp
  • Chicken stock
  • 1.5lb of ground pork
  • 2 large eggs
  • Some type of nuetral oil
  • 5 cloves of garlic
  • Green onions
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. In a large sauté pan, pre-cook the shrimp. Remove them from pan before fully cooked and put in a bowl for later. (don't forget to put the juices in the bowl as well)
  2. add ground pork and garlic to the pan, cook untill browned.
  3. After pork is browned, add chicken stock to the pan. let it heat up then slowly add beaten eggs while stirng the stock slowly.
  4. Now add shrimp and saved juices back into the pan, and let simmer
  5. garnish with green onions.
